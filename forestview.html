KANDLAKOYA OXYGEN PARK

Kandlakoya Oxygen park has been developed into a major urban lung space in the outskirts of Hyderabad, near Medchal town. Government of Telangana developed this space into a model park by installing decorative sculptures apart from the butterfly garden, a gazebo, and a yoga center.

BHAGYANAGAR NANDANAVANAM PARK

The Bhagyanagar Nandanavanam park is an ideal place for citizens to relax and enjoy nature. The park is developed with three-kilometer cycling track, four-kilometer walking track, canopy walkway, and a yoga shed.

SANJEEVAIAH PARK

Deriving its name from the former President of India Dr Neelam Sanjeeva Reddy, this park is spread over an area of 92 acres. Built on the banks of a popular lake, this park offers great landscape views to the patrons visiting here. The fresh air and the lush greenery create splendour everywhere.

LUMBINI PARK

A superb example of craftsmanship, Lumbini Park in Hyderabad offers magnificent experience in all aspects. Spread over an area of 7.5 acres, this park is dedicated to Lord Buddha. From boating to different rides and to sumptuous food; Lumbini park promises a happening time with your friends and family.

INDIRA PARK

A beautiful place in the heart of Hyderabad, Indira Park is said to be inaugurated in the year 1975. A soothing place to relax with your loved ones, Indira Park is known to be one of the best ecologically maintained parks in the city. Sprawling over an area of 76 acres, the atmosphere over here is quite pleasing.
